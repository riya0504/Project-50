Chameleon

Some facts about chameleons
Almost half of all the known chameleon species, live in Madagascar.
They have skin crystals that let them change colour.
The word ‘chameleon’ came from the Greek word ‘Chamai,’ which means earth and ‘Leon’ means lion.
It means “earth lion.”

Camel

Some facts about camels
The camel's hump is like a storage container.
Camels can sleep standing up.
Camels have three eyelids and two layers of eyelashes.
